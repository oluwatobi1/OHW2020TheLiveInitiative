Mission

We are on a RESCUE mission. Our Mission is to :

R - Refine the lives and rewrite the narrative around underprivileged people in Nigerian communities.

E - Empowering and enlightening the less privilege to unlearn redundant environmentally induced traits that have continued to shackle their minds and stifle their development and help them learn to build and forge powerful social interaction skills to enable them to be good leaders and even better followers. As well as learning in-demand 21st century skills So as to

S - Sustainably create a means wealth and individual advancement While also,

C – Creating a ripple change effect in youths and Nigerian communities by inspiring beneficiaries to not only become better versions of themselves but also become change makers who affect the lives of their friends, neighbours, cousins and society at large through responsible leadership, education and enlightenment. And

U – Unearthing their talents to the real world by connecting beneficiaries to a plethora of opportunities to grow their skills and trade. SO AS TO

E – Ensure a better experience for every Nigerian resulting in a more balanced, effective and better society.

Vision

Legendary poet Jonathan Swift once said “Vision is the art of seeing what is invisible to others”.
Many times greatness is invisible, most people think it’s a characteristic that’s only exclusive to a set of privileged people.
At the Live Initiative , we disagree. That’s why our vision is : To make great Heroes and Heroines out of every underprivileged Nigerian.
